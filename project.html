Aims

The aim of my project is to investigate whether cryptocurrency/digital currency actually has a future within the world.
With the popularity and awareness of cryptocurrency increasing worldwide, I wish to investigate whether evidence suggests that cryptocurrency actually has future within society.

Data

Within my project I have used a variety of data
I have scraped data from various websites such as; www.cbdctracker.com, www.tripleai.com. All the csv files which I used for my charts are also available in my Github.
I have also used machine learning technique of cosine similarity within my project to investigate the similarity of bitcoin and the dot com bubble

Challenges Faced

One of the problems which I faced was I was unsure of how to scrape data from a table which had many pages in order to overcome this I copied the html of each page and input it into colab
Then within colab I cleaned each table data individually and then used a function in colab which merged all of them into into one larger csv.
Another problem which I faced was when doing creating the maps vega was not reading my data - in order to overcome this I went into the raw files within github and manually edited the data myself in order for it to match up with the GeoJson

Conclusion

From my research and charts, I have concluded that cryptocurrency does have a future. Despite the price action of the DotCom bubble being of high similarity to crypto. It is important to note that companies such as Amazon were a part of the DotCom bubble. Furthermore, the bubble popping, may actually serve crypto well.
As it may get rid of all the coins which do not have usecases or are scam coins, similarly to how the Dotcom bubble, exposed and exterminated inefficient companies. This would help the lack of trust into cryptocurrency.
However, in order to further cement cryptocurrencies future, I believe it would need more regulation and oversight something which I think is on the horizon as a result of the FTX Scandal. A combination of these things and continuous government adoptions of digital currency & I think crypto currency has a bright future ahead.
If I were to carry out further research I would look at how regulation would impact the cryprocurrency market & also compare the periods of adaption of fiat currency and cryptocurrency, to investigate how full adoption comes about.

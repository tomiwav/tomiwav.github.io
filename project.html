Aims

The aim of my project is to determine the long term viability of cryptocurrency within society.
As cryptocurrency gains increased recognition, it is essential to examine whether or not there is evidence to support its sustained use in the future.

Data

For my project, I have collected & scraped data from a range of sources, including www.cbdctracker.com & www.tripleai.com.
The csv files utilised for my charts are also available on my Github.
In addition, I employed the machine learning technique of cosine similarity to compare the similarity between Bitcoin & the DotCom bubble.

Challenges Faced

One challenge I encountered during my project was figuring out how to extract data from a table with multiple pages. To overcome this, I copied the HTML of each page and input it into Colab.
Afterwards, I cleaned the data from each table seperately and used a function in Colab to merge into a larger CSV.
Another issue I encountered was that Vega was not reading my data when creating maps. To resolve this, I manually edited the data within the raw files to match the GeoJson.

Is it a Bubble

One common critique of cryptocurrency is that it is a bubble & will eventually lose popularity. Using the cosine similarity technique, I found that the price action of cryptocurrency has a 92% similarity to the Dot Com bubble.

Conclusion

In conclusion, my research and analysis I have undertaken suggests that cryptocurrency does have a future. Although the price action of cryptocurrency has a high similarity to the DotCom bubble, it is worth noting that several successful companies, such as Amazon, were also a part of the DotCom bubble
As a matter of fact, the bursting of the bubble may ultimately benefit cryptocurrency by eliminating coins without viable use cases or that are scams, in a similar manner to how the DotCom bubble exposed and eliminated inefficient companies, consequently resulting in increased trust in cryptocurrency.
However, to solidify the future of cryptocurrency, I believe it will need more regulation and oversight, which may come as a result of events like the FTX scandal. With the combination of these factors and continued government adoption of digital currency, I believe cryptocurrency has a bright future.
Further research could examine the impact of regulation on the cryptocurrency market and compare the adoption process of fiat currency and cryptocurrency to understand how widescale adoption may occur.
